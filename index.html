Instructions

1 Hiding behind one of these doors is the ChoreBot.
2 Your mission is to open all of the doors without running into the ChoreBot.
3 If you manage to avoid the ChoreBot until you open the very last door, you win!
4 See if you can score a winning streak!

This web app is based on a concept developed during a web development course and has been improved and enhanced with additional features by Melissa V Herbst.
